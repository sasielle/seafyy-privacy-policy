Privacy Policy for Seafyy

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

Welcome to Seafyy, your trusted seafood delivery service that brings fresh sea items right to your doorstep.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application or website.

3. Data Sharing
